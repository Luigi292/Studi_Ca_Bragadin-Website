Ca' Bragadin Studios

A new approach to business consultancy

Ca' Bragadin Studios was born as an ambitious project, founded by three professionals active in Padua for several decades, with the aim of bringing together in one space all the skills necessary for business consultancy, offering targeted and personalised services for each client.

Inaugurated in December 2024 in the prestigious setting of Palazzo Bragadin, the Studios represent a multidisciplinary hub that integrates chartered accountants, accounting experts and business lawyers, guaranteeing complete support for companies and institutional bodies.

Thanks to the consolidated experience and results achieved in the economic, financial, tax and legal sectors, the founders decided to expand their professional network, creating a shared space available to the local and national business community, to accompany the growth and development of companies.

For every professional need, Ca' Bragadin Studios are available at the headquarters in Via G. Belzoni 180, 35121 Padua.

Mission and Values

What guides us every day

Professional excellence, ethics and support for companies in all phases, ordinary and extraordinary, of their existence: Ca' Bragadin Studios was born with the precise objective of being much more than a simple external consultant for companies, but rather a partner capable of creating tangible added value for its clients thanks to the combination of skills in the legal, tax and corporate fields and the assiduous commitment and dedication of the professionals who collaborate there.

Professional Ethics

Results Orientation
