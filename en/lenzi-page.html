Professional Profile

Founded by Lawyer Maximiliano Lenzi in 1998 - an expert in civil law, specialising in business consulting - Lenzi Practice supports its clients, assisting them daily, with the aim of making business management more efficient and easier.

The timeliness of interventions and maximum availability in solving both small and large issues represent the added value of the service offered. The strength of the Practice is its ability to understand the real needs of the client, offering a personalised service aimed at achieving the best result while respecting the client's needs and budget.

Corporate Contract Law

Complete services for managing commercial contracts, from drafting to negotiation, ensuring maximum protection of business interests at every stage of the contractual relationship.

Lawyer Lenzi offers comprehensive support in managing corporate contract law, with particular attention to dispute prevention and protection of client interests. Each contract is studied to measure to respond to the specific needs of the business, with an approach that combines technical competence and pragmatism.

Main Services

Contract Drafting

Preparation of commercial contracts tailored to every business need, with particular attention to clause clarity and interest protection.

Contract Review

In-depth analysis and modification of existing contracts to ensure maximum protection, identifying potential risks and disadvantageous clauses.

Negotiation

Assistance in contract negotiation to obtain the best conditions, with strategies aimed at maximising benefits for the client.

Collaboration Agreements

Drafting of agreements with collaborators, agents and commercial representatives, balancing the interests of the parties and preventing future disputes.

Supply Contracts

Preparation of agreements with suppliers and subcontractors, with particular attention to quality, delivery and payment clauses.

Safeguard Clauses

Insertion of protective and penalty clauses in contracts, to ensure compliance with obligations and protection in case of default.
