Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2025

1. Introduction

Studio Ca' Bragadin ("we", "our" or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you visit our website (www.studicabragadin.it) and when you contact us through the contact form.

This policy is provided in compliance with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the United Kingdom GDPR and the Italian Code regarding the protection of personal data (Legislative Decree 196/2003 as amended by Legislative Decree 101/2018).

2. Data we collect

Through our website, we may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Contact details: name, surname, email address and telephone number that you provide when you complete the contact form.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone, operating system and other technical information relating to the device you use to access our site.
  • Usage data: information on how you use our website, including pages visited and access times.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries sent through the contact form
  • To provide you with the professional services you have requested
  • To improve our website and your browsing experience
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations

The legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent (for the contact form) and our legitimate interest (for technical and usage data).

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers who assist us in our operations (e.g. website hosting, email services)
  • Competent authorities when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Other professionals in our studio when necessary to provide you with the requested service

All our service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and process data only for the purposes we specify.

5. Data retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contact form data: 24 months from the last contact or until your request for deletion
  • Technical and usage data: 12 months

We may retain data for longer periods when required by law or to defend our legal rights.

6. Your rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: to request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: to ask for inaccurate data to be corrected
  • Right to erasure: to request the deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction: to request the restriction of processing
  • Right to object: to object to the processing of your data
  • Right to data portability: to receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to withdraw consent: to withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, you can contact us at info@studicabragadin.it.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (www.garanteprivacy.it) or the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) if you believe that the processing of your data violates applicable legislation.

7. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Restriction of data access to authorised personnel only
  • Regular backup and recovery procedures

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will publish the updated version on our website with the revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to be informed about how we protect your data.
