Cookie Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2025

1. Definition and regulatory framework

Pursuant to Art. 4 GDPR and Art. 122 of the Italian Privacy Code, "cookie" means any technology that stores or accesses information on the user's device. This policy is drafted in compliance with:

  • EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR)
  • ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC
  • UK Data Protection Act 2018
  • Legislative Decree 196/2003 (Italian Privacy Code) as amended by Legislative Decree 101/2018

2. Types of cookies used

2.1 Strictly necessary cookies (Art. 122 Italian Privacy Code)

This website uses exclusively:

  • Technical session cookies: potentially set by the contact form management system, essential for its proper functioning. These cookies do not require prior consent pursuant to Art. 122 paragraph 1 of the Italian Privacy Code and are automatically deleted at the end of the browsing session.

Legal declaration on the absence of tracking cookies

As verified through certified scanning tools (Cookiebot, Cookieserve), www.studicabragadin.it does not use:

  • Analytical cookies (Art. 122 Italian Privacy Code)
  • Profiling cookies (Art. 122 Italian Privacy Code)
  • Marketing cookies (ePrivacy Directive)

Therefore, no cookie consent banner is required pursuant to GDPR Recital 30 and Art. 122 of the Italian Privacy Code, as there are no cookies that require prior acquisition of consent.

3. Third-party services

The site incorporates the following external services:

  • Google Maps: may set cookies controlled exclusively by Google LLC. These cookies are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: implemented in "privacy-enhanced" mode which does not involve the use of cookies.

Any third-party cookies are not under the control of Studio Ca' Bragadin and are not used for tracking purposes.

4. User rights (Art. 15-22 GDPR)

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Erasure ('right to be forgotten') (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

To exercise these rights, contact the Data Controller at info@studicabragadin.it.

5. Future commitments

Should the website implement diversified cookies (analytical, profiling or marketing) in the future:

  2. A consent acquisition system compliant with Art. 7 GDPR will be implemented
  3. Users will be informed via a specific banner
