Accounting and Financial Statements

Complete management of standard and simplified accounting, with preparation of analytical financial statements, statutory financial statements compliant with current regulations and reclassified financial statements.

The Practice offers a complete service for keeping accounting records, from recording operations to preparing financial statements, with particular attention to the correct representation of the economic, asset and financial situation of the company. Each client benefits from a personalised approach that takes into account the specificities of the sector and company size.

Main Services

Standard accounting

Complete accounting keeping with recording of all operations and related compliance, for companies and individual businesses.

Simplified accounting

Accounting management for small businesses and professionals who can use the simplified regime.

Financial statement preparation

Preparation of statutory financial statements compliant with current regulations, with Notes and Management Reports.

Financial statement analysis

Analysis through ratios and flows to evaluate company performance and identify areas for improvement.

Consolidation

Preparation of consolidated financial statements for corporate groups, with intercompany eliminations and adjustments.

Corporate compliance

Filing of financial statements with the Business Register and other compliance related to the financial year closure.

Tax Services

Complete continuous tax consulting with strategic planning aimed at optimising the tax burden in compliance with current regulations.

The Practice develops personalised tax strategies that take into account the specificities of each client and regulatory developments. The goal is to ensure optimisation of the tax burden while respecting legality, preventing disputes and reducing the risks of assessment.

Main Services

Tax declarations

Preparation and submission of Income Tax Returns (IRES/IRPEF), IRAP, VAT, IMU, with preliminary analysis aimed at optimising the tax burden.

Tax planning

Strategies for legal reduction of the tax burden, with evaluation of different available regimes and options.

Tax assessments

Assistance in all phases of tax assessments, from the investigative phase to litigation.

Voluntary disclosures

Regularisation of omitted payments with reduction of penalties, through the institute of voluntary disclosure.

Tax incentives

Consulting on access to tax credits, with particular reference to those related to investments in instrumental goods, and other tax benefits for businesses.

Business Contract Law

Highly qualified service of drafting, reviewing and assisting in business contract law to support companies in commercial relationships, both in the ordinary phases of activity and on the occasion of extraordinary operations.

Operating with clear, balanced contracts appropriate to the regulatory context is essential to prevent disputes, protect business interests and ensure the legal compliance of operations. The Practice offers support in the preparation of commercial contracts, tender contracts, and corporate contracts, with particular attention to the specific needs of each client and the sector of reference.

Main Services

Commercial contracts

Drafting and review of supply, distribution, agency and commercial representation contracts.

Corporate contracts

Preparation of shareholders' agreements, agreements between shareholders and organisation and functioning regulations.

Tender contracts

Drafting and review of tender contracts for public and private works, with particular attention to civil and tax aspects.

Real estate contracts

Assistance in the preparation of commercial lease contracts, sale and purchase and loan for use.

Collaboration contracts

Drafting of agreements for collaborators, consultants and external professionals.
