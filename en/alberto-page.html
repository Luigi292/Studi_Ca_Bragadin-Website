Accounting and Financial Statements

Assistance in standard and simplified accounting management, preparation of ordinary, abbreviated financial statements and for micro-enterprises, preparation of interim situations and periodic reports, as well as related corporate and tax compliance.

Dr. Cecolin offers a complete service for keeping accounting records, from recording operations to preparing financial statements, with particular attention to the correct representation of the economic, asset and financial situation of the company. Each client benefits from a personalised approach that takes into account the specificities of the sector and company size.

Main Services

Standard accounting

Complete accounting keeping with recording of all operations and related compliance, for companies and individual businesses.

Simplified accounting

Accounting management for small businesses and professionals who can use the simplified regime.

Financial statement preparation

Preparation of statutory financial statements compliant with current regulations, with Notes and Management Reports.

Financial statement analysis

Analysis through ratios and flows to evaluate company performance and identify areas for improvement.

Consolidation

Preparation of consolidated financial statements for corporate groups, with intercompany eliminations and adjustments.

Corporate compliance

Filing of financial statements with the Business Register and other compliance related to the financial year closure.

Taxation and Declarations

Preparation of main tax declarations (Income Tax Return, IRAP, VAT, 770, Unique Certifications) and tax planning activities. Support in relationships with the Tax Administration and assistance during checks and controls.

Dr. Cecolin develops personalised tax strategies that take into account the specificities of each client and regulatory developments. The goal is to ensure optimisation of the tax burden while respecting legality, preventing disputes and reducing the risks of assessment.

Main Services

Tax declarations

Preparation and submission of Income Tax Returns (IRES/IRPEF), IRAP, VAT, IMU, with preliminary analysis aimed at optimising the tax burden.

Tax planning

Strategies for legal reduction of the tax burden, with evaluation of different available regimes and options.

Tax assessments

Assistance in all phases of tax assessments, from the investigative phase to litigation.

Voluntary disclosures

Regularisation of omitted payments with reduction of penalties, through the institute of voluntary disclosure.

Tax incentives

Consulting on access to tax credits and other tax benefits for businesses.

Management Control and Planning

Economic-financial analysis and financial statement ratio analysis, preparation of budgets and business plans, forecast reports and financial planning tools. Also includes business valuation activities aimed at corporate operations or reorganisation processes.

Dr. Cecolin supports companies in defining growth and improvement strategies, through the analysis of economic and financial data and the preparation of planning and control tools. The goal is to provide management with the knowledge tools to make informed decisions and improve business results.

Main Services

Business Plan

Development of industrial and financial plans for business development, with projections and scenarios.

Performance analysis

Monitoring of economic-financial indicators with sector benchmarks and analysis of variances.

Corporate Budget and Forecast

Preparation of annual budgets and multi-year plans, with variance analysis and corrective actions.

Management control

Implementation of management control systems with periodic reporting.

Business valuations

Valuation of company assets for extraordinary operations, successions or generational transitions.

Business Contract Law

Drafting and review of commercial and tender contracts, support in corporate contract law (shareholders' agreements, agreements between shareholders) and in setting up relationships between companies.

Dr. Cecolin offers a highly qualified service of drafting, reviewing and assisting in corporate contract law, aimed at supporting companies in managing commercial relationships, both in the ordinary phases of activity and on the occasion of extraordinary operations. Operating with clear, balanced contracts appropriate to the regulatory context is essential to prevent disputes, protect business interests and ensure the legal compliance of operations.

Main Services

Commercial contracts

Drafting and review of supply, distribution, agency and commercial representation contracts.

Corporate contracts

Preparation of shareholders' agreements, agreements between shareholders and organisation and functioning regulations.

Tender contracts

Drafting and review of tender contracts for public and private works, with particular attention to civil and tax aspects.

Real estate contracts

Assistance in the preparation of commercial lease contracts, sale and purchase and loan for use.

Collaboration contracts

Drafting of agreements for collaborators, consultants and external professionals.

Extraordinary Operations

Assistance in transformations, mergers, splits, contributions and business transfers, leases and corporate reorganisations. Support in generational transitions and due diligence activities related to M&A operations.

Dr. Cecolin offers specialised consulting in extraordinary operations, accompanying companies in all phases of the process: from the initial strategic evaluation, to due diligence, to the structuring of the operation, up to the management of civil, tax and contractual aspects. The goal is to guarantee the success of the operation in compliance with the regulations and in the protection of the interests of all parties involved.

Main Services

Corporate transformations

Assistance in transformation procedures from one corporate form to another, with management of civil and tax aspects.

Mergers and splits

Support in merger and split operations, from due diligence to the drafting of the project.

Business transfers

Assistance in the sale and acquisition of companies, with particular attention to contractual and tax aspects.

Business leases

Support in the preparation and management of business lease contracts, with analysis of civil and tax aspects.

Due diligence

Execution of legal, tax and accounting due diligence activities in preparation for extraordinary operations.

Generational transitions

Support in the planning and implementation of generational transitions, with attention to succession and tax aspects.

Audit and Corporate Governance

Performance of statutory audit activities and statutory auditor in capital companies, preparation of reports and periodic controls. Assistance to corporate bodies (Boards of Directors, boards of statutory auditors) and keeping of corporate books.

Dr. Cecolin offers statutory audit and auditing services, guaranteeing the correctness of accounting information and the compliance of corporate operations with current regulations. The audit activity is carried out in compliance with professional principles and standards, with the aim of providing corporate bodies and interested parties with reasonable assurance on the faithfulness and correctness of the financial statements.

Main Services

Statutory audit of accounts

Execution of accounting audit according to national and international auditing principles, with issuance of the auditor's report.

Board of statutory auditors

Assumption of positions as statutory auditor or substitute, with carrying out of controls on the regular keeping of accounting.

Periodic controls

Execution of periodic controls on corporate management, with verification of the compliance of operations with the statute and the law.

Keeping corporate books

Keeping of mandatory corporate books (share register, meeting minutes book, board minutes book) and conservation of corporate documentation.

Assistance to corporate bodies

Support to the Board of Directors and the Assembly in the correct application of corporate regulations and in the drafting of minutes.

Reporting irregularities

Reporting of irregularities and violations found during control activities to the competent bodies.
